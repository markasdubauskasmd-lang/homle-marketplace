Scan stage 2

Speak or type the instructions

Choose the current room above, then speak naturally. Tideway adds a visible room marker to the notes when listening starts; change the current room as you walk and the next speech stays grouped correctly. You can still say “this is the kitchen” or “we’re moving into the bathroom”, then describe the work or what you see, such as “the floor needs mopping” or “there is grease around the hob.” Say “do not”, “no need” or “skip” for anything the Cleaner must leave alone. Tideway keeps those exclusions as clear instructions, turns supported conditions into direct tasks and removes repeated bullets for your review.
