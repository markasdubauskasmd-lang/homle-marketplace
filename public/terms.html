Pilot terms

Draft for review: these terms deliberately do not pretend the operator, insurance, cancellation policy or cleaner engagement model has been finalised. Those decisions and legal details are required before accepting live bookings.

Requests are not bookings

Submitting a form only asks Tideway to review a cleaning need or cleaner application. It does not create a confirmed booking, guarantee work, or authorise a payment.

The private request tracker reports only stages recorded by Tideway. A tracker stage, room-scan review, quote or cleaner response does not create a booking; confirmation requires the protected booking pack described below.

How a job becomes confirmed

A job is only confirmed when Tideway gives the customer the agreed scope, date, price and cancellation terms, gives an approved cleaner the scope, date and proposed pay, the full visit remains inside an explicitly confirmed cleaner availability window, both separately accept, the booking checks pass, and Tideway records the final protected booking pack. If a cleaner declines, availability is withdrawn or Tideway withdraws a proposal before confirmation, the affected private links become read-only and Tideway must recheck the match. A pre-booking withdrawal does not itself create a booking, take payment or alter a confirmed booking.

Changes, cancellation and safety reports

A request submitted from a private booking pack records an item for Tideway to review. It does not itself cancel, reschedule, change access, alter the scope, authorise a refund or replace the confirmed booking pack. Until Tideway records and separately confirms an agreed change, the existing booking remains the current record. Urgent danger should be reported through emergency services or the verified Tideway support route as appropriate, not only through the online queue.

Cleaner applications

Applying does not guarantee work and does not require an applicant to accept an opportunity. Before any work is offered, Tideway must complete the checks appropriate to the final engagement model and explain the proposed pay, status, responsibilities and payment timing.

Property access and job information

Customers are expected to provide accurate information about the site, hazards, access, parking, alarms, pets and any specialist requirements. Cleaners should only carry out the agreed checklist and should report unsafe or materially different conditions.

Customers who add property photos or dictated instructions must have permission to share them, avoid unnecessary personal or security information, and review the generated checklist for accuracy. A photo job brief remains a draft until Tideway and both sides agree the final scope.

Price, cancellation and complaints

The final quote, included work, payment timing, cancellation rules, re-clean process and complaint route must be supplied before a live job is accepted. No pricing displayed during the development pilot is binding.

Identity and contact

The legal name, address and verified contact details of the Tideway operator must be added before public launch.
